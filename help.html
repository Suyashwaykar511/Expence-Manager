Expense Manager Help

1. How do I add income?
Click "Add Income", enter date, description, amount, and save.
2. How do I add expenses?
Click "Add Expense", enter date, description, amount, and save.
3. Where can I see my transactions?
All transactions appear in the Transaction History table below your summary cards.
4. How is my balance calculated?
Balance = Total Income - Total Expenses. This updates automatically with each transaction.
5. Can I edit or delete transactions?
Currently no, but you can add a correcting transaction with the opposite amount.
6. How do I categorize expenses?
Include categories in descriptions (e.g., "Food: Groceries") for now. Proper categories coming soon.
7. Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted and never shared with third parties.
8. Can I export my data?
Export feature coming in the next update (version 1.2).
